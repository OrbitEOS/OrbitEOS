# Product Overview

OrbitEOS is an energy management platform that monitors and controls solar panels, batteries, grid connections, and EV chargers in real time. It is built for the AI era — natively integrated with AI assistants through the Model Context Protocol (MCP).

## Key Capabilities

### Live Energy Dashboard
Real-time power flow visualization showing every watt moving through your system — solar production, battery state of charge, grid import/export, and consumption.

### AI-Powered Control
Ask questions and give commands in natural language through Claude, ChatGPT, or any MCP-compatible AI assistant. No dashboards or buttons needed — just talk to your energy system.

### Battery Intelligence
Smart charge and discharge optimization that learns your patterns and maximizes self-consumption and battery lifespan. Automated scheduling for off-peak grid charging.

### Grid Optimization
Peak shaving, self-consumption maximization, and demand response — automatically reduce your electricity costs with intelligent control strategies.

### Multi-Site Management
Hierarchical site tree with role-based access control. Manage regions, hubs, sites, and zones from a single pane of glass. Built for portfolios of any size.

### Plugin Marketplace
Extend OrbitEOS with plugins for device drivers, analytics modules, control strategies, notifications, and dashboard widgets — install in one click.

## Energy Flow

OrbitEOS visualizes and controls the flow of energy across four key nodes:

- **Solar** — PV production from rooftop or ground-mounted panels
- **Battery** — Energy storage systems with state-of-charge tracking
- **Grid** — Utility grid connection with import and export metering
- **Home / Load** — Total consumption from all connected loads

The platform acts as a smart hub, routing energy between these nodes based on optimization rules, time-of-use tariffs, and AI-driven strategies.

## How It Works

1. **Connect** — Link your energy system: inverters, meters, batteries, EV chargers. Supports all major brands through the marketplace.
2. **Monitor** — See real-time energy flows, historical analytics, and system health. Set alerts and track performance across all your sites.
3. **Optimize** — Let AI assistants analyze your energy patterns, suggest optimizations, and control your system — all through natural conversation.

## Platform Architecture

- **Transport** — Streamable HTTP (MCP)
- **Auth** — OAuth 2.1 with PKCE
- **API** — REST + WebSocket + MCP
- **Edge** — OpenEMS-based edge controllers
- **AI** — Native MCP integration
