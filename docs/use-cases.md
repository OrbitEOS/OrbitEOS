# Use Cases

OrbitEOS scales from a single home to entire energy communities.

## Residential

**Maximize solar self-consumption**

Smart battery management learns your household patterns and optimizes when to store, use, or export energy — reducing your electricity bill automatically.

- Monitor solar production and household consumption in real time
- Automated battery charge/discharge scheduling
- Off-peak grid charging for maximum savings
- Natural language control via AI assistants

## Commercial

**Reduce peak demand charges**

Peak shaving, load management, and grid optimization strategies that cut commercial electricity costs. Multi-site fleet view for building portfolios.

- Peak shaving to reduce demand charges
- Load management across multiple buildings
- Fleet-wide energy analytics and reporting
- Role-based access for facility managers and energy teams

## Energy Communities

**Manage distributed assets at scale**

Hierarchical site management with role-based access. Aggregate data across hundreds of sites, manage sub-accounts, and optimize fleet-wide performance.

- Hierarchical site tree: regions, hubs, sites, zones
- Sub-account management for tenants and partners
- Aggregated analytics across the entire portfolio
- Marketplace plugins for community-specific control strategies
